Let's Build!

The MK Guitars Shop represents our highest level of hand-made custom guitars. Each custom order is designed by you.

Follow the steps bellow to request.

1) Subscribe to our website.
2) Send us a custom order quote request. Email us a picture of the guitar you would like, along with below details.
In order to work up an accurate price, please include the following with your quote request....
Body Style/Color
Body Wood (Mahogany, Ash, Basswood or Alder)
Body Top Wood (Flamed, Quilted, or Spalted Maple or None Required)
Body Binding Color (if adding Top Wood) - Black, White or 5-Ply B/W
Neck Wood - Maple (natural or vintage tint) Bolt On or Set Neck
Headstock - Corvette, Screamin' Eagle or Round Top LP
Headstock Logo - Includes "Custom Build" or No Logo
Fretboard - Maple, Rosewood or Ebony with Dot, Block or Trap Inlay Markers
Fretboard Radius - 10" or 14" (compound radius and custom frets not available - Standard frets are Meduim or Jumbo)
Tuners - Vintage, Modern or Locking
Hardware - Chrome, Black, Gold
Pickup Configuration
Bridge Style - Tremolo, Hardtail, Ashtray, Bigsby Style, Wraparound
Gig Bag or Hard Case ​

.....The more information we have the better! Copy and paste this list into the request e-mail with your selections and any additional details.
Custom Orders typically take 8-10 weeks. We require the cost of your guitar as a deposit at time of order approval. We will send you detailed completion photos for your approval prior to shipping.
Not what you expected? We will refund your deposit minus a $50 custom order fee.
All custom orders include a free setup and shipping.
